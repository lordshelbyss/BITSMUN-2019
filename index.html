    • LETTER FROM SECRETARY-GENERAL

    To all future delegates, esteemed guests and friends.

    On behalf of the secretariat of BITSMUN ‘19, it is my extreme pleasure and honour to invite you all to the 8 annual BITSMUN Hyderabad from October 18 to 20 at the Birla Institute of Technology and Sciences, Hyderabad.Throughout the years BITSMUN has sought to empower minds from across the nation, through endearing and competitive debate. The conference brings together delegates to not only discuss international issues, but encourage delegates to take part in further debates and MUNs, learning to share their ideas while working together with others to reach an objective while tackling the worldwide problems of today.Since its inception, the priority at BITSMUN Hyderabad has always been built on quality debate, competitive and engaging committees, a platform for delegates to share their ideas, but most of all as an experienced worth remembering. It is the legacy and reputation created by previous editions that we hope to live up to if not to exceed.

    With a diverse set of committees being The United Nations General Assembly, The Human Rights Council, The United Nations Security Council, Continuous Crisis Committee and The Organisation of Islamic Cooperation, we hope that delegates will foster research, debating and negotiating skills to not only help themselves but inspire others to think critically about current affairs and international relations.As a conference, we wish that delegates go out with enhanced knowledge and skill, licking their lips at the next opportunity that is presented to them and to be the platform where one will begin or expand on that journey, while having an exceptional time at our conference.The Organizing committee of BITSMUN ‘19, I once again would whole heartedly invite you all to be a part of three days of vigorous debate, perspicacious and judicious negotiation and an experience to last a lifetime!



    Sincerely,
    Tejas Vaid
    Secretary-General, BITSMUN ‘19
